Ingredients

  • A hotdog bun
  • A hotdog
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Relish

Steps

  1. Grab the hotdog and rip it open
  2. Place the hotdog bun within the hotdog
  3. Rub the ketchup on the hotdog
  4. Rub the mustard on the ketchup
  5. Rub the relish on the outside of the hotdog
  6. Enjoy!
